# Healthcare Claims Analysis

**Project Goal:** Analyze patient admissions, claims, and billing data to identify trends and insights that can improve hospital operations and revenue cycle management.

## Dataset
- Source: [Kaggle Healthcare Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/prasad22/healthcare-dataset)
- Includes: patient demographics, medical conditions, hospital visits, billing amounts, insurance providers, and doctor assignments.

## Tools
- MySQL (queries and database setup)
- Excel/Tableau (optional visualization)

## Key Analysis
1. **Total Patients by Hospital**
   - Identified hospitals with highest patient load for resource planning.
2. **Average Billing by Medical Condition**
   - Highlighted which conditions contribute most to revenue.
3. **Admissions by Gender**
   - Analyzed demographic distribution of patient visits.
4. **Top 5 Doctors by Patient Volume**
   - Determined workload distribution across physicians.
5. **Average Billing by Insurance Provider**
   - Insights on insurance-based revenue trends.

## Skills Practiced
- SQL: SELECT, WHERE, JOIN, GROUP BY, ORDER BY, aggregates
- Data interpretation & reporting
- Healthcare operations analysis

## Insights
- Emergency departments and certain conditions generate the most claims.
- Some insurance providers are associated with higher average billing.
- Patient volume varies significantly across doctors and hospitals.
